Here is a complete, clear, and structured breakdown comparing the best database and storage solutions for hosting videos. 

As you noted, keeping Logins (Auth) and Images/Metadata in the Supabase Free Tier is a brilliant, zero-cost architecture. Traditional databases are terrible at storing large video files, so we use Object Storage or Video CDN services instead.

Below is the cost breakdown for the best video hosting options, calculated for a typical course platform setup:
*   Video Storage: 100 GB of video files (approx. 50-100 high-quality videos)
*   Video Delivery (Egress/Bandwidth): 500 GB of video streamed by students per month

---

### 1. The Absolute Cheapest (DIY): Cloudflare R2
Cloudflare R2 is an Amazon S3 alternative that is famous for having zero egress (bandwidth) fees. It is exceptionally suited for your current React setup because your app already supports playing raw video files (`.mp4`) directly inside standard HTML5 video elements.

*   Free Tier Includes: 10 GB of free storage.
*   Storage Cost: $0.015 / GB per month.
*   Egress (Bandwidth) Cost: $0.00 (Free, unlimited streaming traffic).
*   Monthly Cost Calculation:
    *   Storage (100 GB): (100 GB - 10 GB Free) * $0.015 = $1.35
    *   Traffic (500 GB): $0.00
*   Total Cost: 
    *   Monthly: $1.35 USD (approx. ₹110)
    *   Annually: $16.20 USD (approx. ₹1,350)

Why it's the best for you: It integrates seamlessly with your existing Supabase code as an external storage link, and your monthly bill is basically $1.

---

### 2. The Best Managed Value: Bunny.net Stream
Bunny.net Stream is a dedicated video streaming CDN. Unlike Cloudflare R2, it automatically transcodes (encodes) your uploaded videos into multiple resolutions (like YouTube does) so they play smoothly even for students with slow mobile internet connections.

*   Encoding/Transcoding: Free (Unlimited uploads).
*   Storage Cost: $0.01 / GB per month.
*   Traffic/Egress Cost: ~$0.005 / GB streamed (dynamic global routing rates).
*   Monthly Cost Calculation:
    *   Storage (100 GB): 100 GB * $0.01 = $1.00
    *   Traffic (500 GB): 500 GB * $0.005 = $2.50
*   Total Cost:
    *   Monthly: $3.50 USD (approx. ₹290)
    *   Annually: $42.00 USD (approx. ₹3,500)

Why it's the best managed value: It automatically handles video compression, fast global streaming (so videos load instantly without buffering), and video security locks to prevent students from downloading or sharing your paid courses.

---

### 3. The Premium Enterprise Option: Cloudflare Stream
Cloudflare Stream is an all-in-one managed video platform. Unlike R2, it has its own built-in video encoding pipelines and players. It is calculated by "minutes of video" instead of gigabytes.

*   Storage Cost: $5.00 per 1,000 minutes stored per month.
*   Delivery Cost: $1.00 per 1,000 minutes streamed.
*   Monthly Cost Calculation (assuming ~6,000 minutes of courses stored & ~30,000 minutes watched):
    *   Storage: 6,000 mins = $30.00
    *   Traffic: 30,000 mins = $30.00
*   Total Cost:
    *   Monthly: $60.00 USD (approx. ₹5,000)
    *   Annually: $720.00 USD (approx. ₹60,000)

---

### 4. Why Supabase Pro Storage is NOT Recommended for Video
While Supabase is amazing for data and logins, using their paid storage tiers for high-traffic video streaming gets expensive fast due to their standard cloud egress rates.

*   Base Subscription: $25.00 / month (includes 8 GB storage, 50 GB egress).
*   Extra Storage Cost: $0.021 / GB.
*   Extra Egress (Bandwidth) Cost: $0.09 / GB.
*   Monthly Cost Calculation:
    *   Base Plan: $25.00
    *   Storage (100 GB): (100 GB - 8 GB Base) * $0.021 = $1.93
    *   Traffic (500 GB): (500 GB - 50 GB Base) * $0.09 = $40.50
*   Total Cost:
    *   Monthly: $67.43 USD (approx. ₹5,600)
    *   Annually: $809.16 USD (approx. ₹67,000)

---

### 🏆 Recommendation Summary

1. **Choose Cloudflare R2 ($16 / year)** if you want the absolute cheapest, bare-bones solution. You upload the MP4 files, put the URL in Supabase, and play them directly. It costs almost nothing.
2. **Choose Bunny.net Stream ($42 / year)** if you want a premium student experience with **no buffering** (adaptive resolution), a beautiful custom video player, and built-in security to prevent course piracy.
