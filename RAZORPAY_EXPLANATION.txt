Why do we need to do the Webhook steps?

Think of the Webhook as an invisible, secure phone line between Razorpay and your database. 

1. A student goes to your website and pays 1000 Rupees.
2. The money goes into Razorpay.
3. Here is the problem: How does your website know the student actually paid so it can unlock the course videos for them? If a hacker fakes a payment, we don't want to give them the course for free.
4. The Solution (The Webhook): The moment Razorpay receives the real money, Razorpay automatically calls that webhook link and uses the Secret Password (FluteSecureWebhook2026!) to prove it's really them. Razorpay says: "I just collected the money! Unlock the course for this student!"

If we don't set up the webhook with that password, Razorpay has no way to securely tell your website that the payment was successful. The money would leave the student's bank account, but they wouldn't get access to the course!
